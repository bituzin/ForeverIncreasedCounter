What is Forever Increased Counter

i
Forever Increased Counter is a simple DApp running on the Celo blockchain that allows users to increment a counter through on-chain transactions. Each increase is permanently recorded on the blockchain, creating an immutable history of all interactions.

How to Use

i
  1. Click "Connect Wallet" to connect your Celo wallet
  2. Once connected, use the "Increase" button to increment the counter
  3. Your transaction will be confirmed on the Celo blockchain
  4. All increase transactions are recorded in the history
  5. You can disconnect your wallet at any time
